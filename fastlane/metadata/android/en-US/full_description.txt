Known Issues:

Twitch Streams:
– Twitch stream playback works only on devices with Google WebView installed.
– On some devices (for example, the Honor Pad 8), it’s not possible to play multiple Twitch streams with sound at the same time.
On such devices, switching audio between stream players may cause streams to pause.
To avoid this, it’s recommended to mute other streams before enabling sound on the one you want to listen to.
